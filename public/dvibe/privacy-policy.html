Privacy Policy

Date: 31 Dec 2024

Thank you for choosing DVibe. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Email address: Collected when you register or contact us for support.
  • Account-related information: Tokens, rewards, and quiz progress.

1.2 Usage Information

  • Interaction data: Includes quiz participation, ad views, and spins.
  • Device information: Such as your device type and operating system.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve app functionalities.
  • Enable participation in quizzes, token accumulation, and rewards.
  • Deliver ads to unlock quizzes.
  • Respond to support requests and account deletion forms.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. Ads and Rewards

To unlock quizzes, users are required to view ads. Tokens earned from quizzes can be used for spins that may result in coupons or other rewards.

4. Account Deletion

Users can delete their accounts by:

  • Filling out the account deletion form available on our website.
  • Upon submission, your account will be deleted immediately.

For assistance, contact us at dvibe@devskrew.com.

5. Data Retention

We retain user data as long as it is necessary to provide services. Upon account deletion, all personal information will be permanently erased.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • When required by law.
  • To trusted service providers assisting in app operations.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be communicated within the app or via email.
